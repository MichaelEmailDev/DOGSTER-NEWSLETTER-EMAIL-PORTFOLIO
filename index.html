Hyperthyroidism in Dogs: Causes, Signs & Management. Hyperthyroidism in dogs, though rare, bears serious consequences. Recognizing associated signs early is crucial. Dogs possess a thyroid gland producing hormones; excessive release causes adverse effects. While uncommon overall, it's most prevalent in older dogs. Cancerous thyroid masses are a common cause, alongside benign adenomas or excessive levothyroxine.
